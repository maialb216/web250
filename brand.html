Brand

The concept of the brand is a bakery that specializes in baked goods that utilize real, edible flowers in both the baking and the decoration of the goods.

The brand colors and fonts will be, as follows:

  • Thistle for page backgrounds
  • Lavender for div backgrounds, as seen in this box
  • Playfair in Indigo for more basic text
  • Prata in Medium Purple for special text, such as headers
